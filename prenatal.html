Prenatal Support

The prenatal appointment will give you the information you need to start your breastfeeding journey with confidence.

Note: All visits take place in the comfort of your own home.

This visit includes:

In-depth maternal history and breast assessment

Discussion of your breastfeeding goals

Understanding what to expect in the first days.

Education including, but not limited to:

  • Bonding and feeding in the immediate postpartum period
  • Feeding in the first days
  • Establishment and maintenance of milk supply
  • Increasing milk supply
  • Normal newborn behavior and feeding patterns
  • Preventing and managing sore nipples
  • Latching techniques
  • Diaper counts
  • Sleep patterns
  • Managing engorgement

Prenatal appointments (90 minutes) are $140. If your insurance policy is not in network, you will be billed up front and provided with a superbill to submit to your insurance for reimbursement.
