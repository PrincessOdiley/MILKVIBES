About Stephanie Simon

Stephanie graduated from YSU with a Bachelor’s of Science in Nursing in 2008. Since then, she has become a Certified Breastfeeding Specialist since and an Internationally Board Certified Lactation Consultant (IBCLC).

She spent her nursing career working with infants, children, and mothers with various diagnoses and feeding needs. She’s had experience in the NICU, Infant/Toddler Unit, and outpatient pediatrics.
